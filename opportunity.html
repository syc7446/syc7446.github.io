Open Positions

Thank you for your interest in working with me! I have multiple openings to join the Algorithmic Robotics Group (ARG) at NTU Singapore. You can explore the broad research interests of our group in the About section and find additional details in the Publications section. Kindly note that due to the volume of applications, I may not be able to respond to everyone individually. Please read the following instructions carefully and follow them accordingly. Thank you for your understanding!


PhD student positions

I am actively seeking self-motivated students to join in Spring or Fall 2026, with a strong background in robotics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. To apply to join the group, please complete this Google form. I will review submissions on a rolling basis, and only applicants selected for further consideration will be invited to proceed with the formal application process via the NTU portal. General information about the PhD application process is available on the NTU CCDS website.


Postdoc positions

I am looking to hire one postdoc starting in Spring 2026 to work on epistemic robot learning, jointly with Alan Siu Lun Chau. Please see the postdoc fellowship opportunity here, and the job description and project details here. To apply for this opportunity, please complete this Google form.


Master’s student and intern positions

If you are an NTU undergraduate or master’s student, please email your CV with the subject line: "[Master’s Student (or Intern) Application] Your Name – Your Department."

If you are from another university and interested in pursuing a master’s degree, please apply through the college first. Application instructions can be found here. Please email me after you have been accepted by the college.

If you are from another university and interested in internships, opportunities are available year-round under certain conditions. Please complete this Google form. I will review submissions on a rolling basis, and only applicants selected for further consideration will be invited for an interview.
